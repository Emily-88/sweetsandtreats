Sweets & Treats History

Constantly inspired by my mother, I began baking at a very young age. This shared passion for baking brought my mom and I closer together throughout the years, and she was biggest supporter when I first mentioned the idea of starting a bakery. Finally, after years of planning, I proudly opened Sweets & Treats on February 22, 2022. Since then, we've had some struggles, especially with the pandemic, but thankfully we pulled through and are thriving.

Here's to many more years!
