How to Make Cake Flour

Ever get halfway through a recipe and then realize you're out of something? That's happened to me plenty of times, despite my mother constantly telling me to fully read the recipe before baking.

While I can't always find a perfect substitute, I did discover you can make a pretty good cake flour with just all-purpose flour and some cornstarch!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Cornstarch

Ratio

  • Take 1 full cup of all purpose flour, and remove 2 tablespoons from it (I like to dump the flour into a clean bowl to scoop out the tablespoons; it's less messy)
  • Replace the removed flour with cornstarch
  • Ta-da! You now have 1 cup of cake flour!
